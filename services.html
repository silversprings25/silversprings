Pool Cleaning & Maintenance

Our routine pool cleaning service is designed for consistent, comfortable water and a great-looking pool. Choose weekly or biweekly pool cleaning based on your pool’s usage, sun exposure, and debris level.

Weekly & Biweekly Pool Cleaning

Set-it-and-forget-it pool service that helps prevent algae, cloudy water, and chemical swings—especially during Arizona heat.

Skimming, Brushing & Vacuuming

  • Skimming surface debris
  • Brushing walls and steps
  • Vacuuming as needed for fine debris

Tile & Surface Cleaning

We help keep tile lines and surfaces looking their best by reducing buildup from sunscreen, dust, and mineral residue over time.

Skimmer & Pump Basket Emptying

Clearing baskets improves circulation, protects your equipment, and supports cleaner filtration.

Water Chemistry & Balancing

Balanced water isn’t just about clarity—it’s about safety, comfort, and protecting your pool surfaces and equipment. We test, adjust, and maintain water chemistry for swim-ready results.

Precise Water Testing

Accurate testing is the foundation of pool care. We track water chemistry to prevent irritation, algae, and damage caused by imbalances.

Chemical Balancing for Safety & Comfort

We aim for water that feels good on skin and eyes while staying safe for swimmers and pets.

Chlorine, pH & Alkalinity Adjustments

  • Sanitizer level support (chlorine)
  • pH adjustment for comfort and protection
  • Total alkalinity support to stabilize water

Salt System Care

Salt pools still require maintenance. We help monitor salt system performance, support clean cell operation, and keep water balanced.

Repair & Equipment Service

Pool equipment problems can turn into expensive issues if ignored. We inspect, troubleshoot, and handle minor repairs so your system runs efficiently and reliably.

Pump & Filter Inspections

We check for common wear-and-tear issues and signs of reduced performance to catch problems early.

Equipment Troubleshooting & Minor Repairs

Not sure why your pool is cloudy or circulation is weak? We help identify the cause and recommend the next best step.

Filter Cleaning & Replacements

Filters are the heart of clean water. We clean filters when needed and can replace cartridges/media to restore filtration performance.

Pool Equipment Upgrades & Installations

Upgrading pumps, filters, or related components can improve energy efficiency and water quality. We can help install and set up new equipment.

Note: We focus on troubleshooting and minor repairs. If a repair requires specialized work, we’ll explain the issue clearly and help you plan the next step.
