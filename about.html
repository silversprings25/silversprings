Who We Are

Silver Springs Pool Care is a Veteran owned, we help homeowners enjoy clean, clear pools without the stress. We provide reliable pool cleaning, weekly and biweekly pool service, and water chemistry balancing for residential pools across Mesa, Gilbert, and surrounding East Valley communities in Arizona.

Our goal is simple: show up on schedule, handle the details, and communicate clearly—so you can relax and enjoy your backyard oasis.

What You Can Expect

Consistent, Reliable Service

Weekly or biweekly visits to keep your pool swim-ready and looking great.

Comfortable, Balanced Water

Precise testing and adjustments to help maintain safe, comfortable water.

Equipment Support

Filter care, equipment checks, and troubleshooting for common pool issues.
